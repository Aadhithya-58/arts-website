TANJORE PAINTINGS

Tanjore or Thanjavur paintings are the traditional art forms of Tamilnadu. They mostly depict pictures of Gods and Goddesses decorated with 22 carat gold foil and semi precious stones. The paintings are framed with authentic teak wood frames.
